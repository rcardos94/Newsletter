Chaos Strikes

Local squirrels have staged a hostile takeover of the neighborhood bird feeders, sending birds into an uproar. Eyewitnesses report the squirrels working in teams, using advanced acorn-lobbing tactics. "We never saw it coming," said one bewildered blue jay. Residents are advised to secure their snacks and remain indoors until further notice. In unrelated news, local dogs seem unusually excited about today's developments. Stay tuned for updates!.

Breaking News

Chaos erupted in Kennesaw after a mysterious shipment of 10,000 tons of nuts accidentally landed in Swift Cantrell Park, causing local squirrels to grow to massive sizes. The giant squirrels have since taken over playgrounds, houses, schools, and even Kennesaw Hall. Attempts to appease the squirrels with peanut butter sandwiches have failed, as the rodents now appear to be running the city. People are loving it, but officials are scrambling to regain control.
